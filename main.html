About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I have been working on high-dimensional statistical inference advised by Larry Wasserman and Sivaraman Balakrishnan. I am also lucky to have worked with Aaditya Ramdas, Ann Lee, Jing Lei and Peter Freeman during my Ph.D.
